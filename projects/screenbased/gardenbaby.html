Journey Through the Garden of Sins

2023
Interactive Sculpture
VR Game

This interactive project explores a digital metaverse of sin-inspired stations as a digital baby, celebrating imperfection and randomness in AI data of the Internet human behaviors.

This interactive project takes viewers on a sarcastic journey through a digital metaverse filled with seven sin-inspired stations, each constructed from human comments gathered via AI. The experience challenges the notion of "bad" data, celebrating the creativity found in randomness and flaws. Through this journey, participants unlock an unprocessed digital creature, sparking conversations about digital evolution and imperfection. Presented at Dutch Design Week.
