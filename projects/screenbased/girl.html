? Girl

2023
Mixed Media
Augmented Reality

This series follows a devil girls's journey through emotional scars, transforming into a bald demon symbolizing pain and resilience, exploring healing and the contradictions between personal growth and societal expectations, all in augmented reality.

This series delves into the life of a young woman navigating a world that inflicts emotional scars. Her desires transform her into a bald demon, a symbolic representation of the pain and resilience born from rejection and hurt. The work reflects on the healing journey, using bold imagery to highlight the contradictions within personal growth and societal expectations.
