Freakshow 2045

2024
Animation
2’11’’

Freak Show 2045 examines beauty, otherness, and genetic manipulation, questioning humanity's future through dance and unsettling subtitles.

Freakshow 2045 explores the interplay of otherness and beauty in a future defined by genetic manipulation. A woman of color performs an "exotic" dance to "oriental" music, referencing colonial-era commodification of marginalized bodies. Subtitles provoke the question: is she the "freak," or are unmodified humans the anomaly in a world of genetic engineering? Set in a future where “designer babies” are reserved for the wealthy, the work envisions natural bodies as symbols of imperfection and exclusion, raising unsettling questions about the cost of humanity in the pursuit of perfection.
