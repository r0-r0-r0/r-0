Fantasy of Nipples

2022
Ceramics
Augmented Reality

This project uncovers the hidden voices of nipples through ceramics and AR, blending humor and critique.

This project explores the societal suppression of nipples, particularly women’s, as a metaphor for overlooked forms of communication and expression. Using ceramics and augmented reality, the work creates intricate forms that “speak” through their shapes. A smartphone app allows viewers to uncover the hidden dialogue of these often-silenced signals, blending physical and digital elements to challenge societal norms.
